# SportsSwap Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

## What we collect

- **Account info**: Your email address and username when you create an account.
- **Profile data**: Profile photo, sports you play, and bio if you choose to add them.
- **User content**: Listings, posts, achievements, comments, and messages you create in the app.
- **Photos**: Images you upload to listings, posts, and your profile.

## How we use it

We use your data to operate the app — showing your listings to buyers, displaying your posts in the community feed, and enabling chat between users. We do not sell your data to third parties.

## Data storage

All data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure. Your data is stored in the United States.

## Deleting your account

To delete your account and all associated data, email us at SportsMACKAY@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at SportsMACKAY@gmail.com.
